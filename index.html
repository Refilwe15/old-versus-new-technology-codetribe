OLD VERSUS NEW TECHNOLOGY

On this website you will learn or see how technology has changed from past decades.

THE OLD WAY

THE NEW WAY

Flip Phone

Flip phones were small and strong phones used for calling and texting, with buttons, long battery life, simple cameras, and basic games.

Smartphone

Smartphones are modern phones used for calling, texting, taking photos, using apps, playing games, and browsing the internet.

Files

Files were used to keep paper documents safe and organized in offices and homes.

Cloud Storage

Cloud storage is a way to save and access files online using the internet, without needing paper.

Fax Machine

Fax machines were used to send and receive paper documents through telephone lines.

Online Fax Services

Online fax services let people send and receive documents using the internet without a fax machine.

Old Computer

Old computers were big and slow, used mainly for simple tasks like typing and calculations.

New Computer

New computers are faster and smaller, able to do many things like gaming, browsing, and working on the internet.
